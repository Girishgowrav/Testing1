About the event

Robo Football League is a collegiate robotics competition that combines system design, embedded control, and competitive strategy. Teams design robots to score goals in a dynamic arena while adhering to safety and design constraints.

Event goals

Encourage practical engineering, teamwork and innovation. Provide a platform for students to showcase robotics skills and compete under standardized rules.

Format

Two categories — Singles (3 members) and Doubles (6 members). Matches run in timed rounds with referees monitoring for rule compliance.
